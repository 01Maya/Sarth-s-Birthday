

# 🎉 Celebrate – Elegant Birthday Invitation Website  

An elegant, joyful, and interactive **birthday invitation website** crafted with soft pastel gradients, playful animations, and a luxurious UI.  
Built using **Next.js**, **Framer Motion**, and **Tailwind CSS**, this site combines celebration and modern design into one elegant experience.  

---

## ✨ Features  

- 🎈 **Interactive Hero Section** – Smooth animations, gradient heading, and elegant confetti.  
- 💌 **Event Details Section** – Displays What, When, and Where with soft glass effects.  
- 🎁 **Scratch & Reveal** – Interactive cards with surprise emojis and joyful messages.  
- ⏳ **Countdown Timer** – Live countdown until the celebration with glowing effects.  
- 📍 **Location & Contact** – Venue, address, and contact details in frosted cards.  
- 💌 **Closing Message** – Heartfelt thank-you note with RSVP/share icons.  
- ✨ **Confetti Replay Button** – Replays confetti anytime without resetting.  
- 🖱️ **Custom Cursor Trail** – Shimmering golden-white luxury cursor with smooth motion.  
- ☁️ **Floating Elements** – Subtle pastel shapes for depth and charm.  
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** – Seamlessly adapts to all screen sizes.  
- 💫 **Smooth Animations** – Elegant transitions throughout the website.

## 🧩 Section Details

Soft pastel gradient background, animated “You’re Invited!” heading, and floating confetti.
